RULES:

1. Induvidual participants.

2. The event consist of 3 rounds.

3. Languages are C,C++,HTML,JAVA,PYTHON.

ROUND 1:(multiple choice questions)

    • In this round the participants will be provided with 20 multiple choice questions.
      Participants must score 12 out of 20 to enter the next round.

    • ROUND 2:(Error debugging)

    • In this round the participants will be provided with 5 questions and marks will be alotted depending upon the number of bugs found in the respective program.

    • ROUND 3:(program based on real time example)

    • In this round the participants will be provided with one real time question. program can be developed in any on the 5 languages.
      round 3 is an optional one if the number of participants selected from the second round are less, then this round will not be conducted.

    • Timings:

    • First round will be conducted for 10 minutes. 30 seconds is alotted for each question.

      second round will be conducted for 20 mins

      If third round is conducted , it will be for 20 minutes.
